## 1. Why This Scenario (Context Anchoring)

The corporate wellness industry in India is a real, active market. GOQii Technologies
Pvt Ltd — a Mumbai-based preventive-healthcare technology company (Founder & CEO:
Vishal Gondal) — runs an enterprise wellness business whose real, publicly documented
offerings include:

- **GOQii Corporate Challenge (GCC):** a real inter-corporate fitness event combining
  daily virtual sessions, spot challenges, and a multi-week inter-corporate step
  challenge inside the GOQii app. The 2021–22 edition drew **over 25,000 employees
  across ~40 corporations** (IOCL, HPCL, Tata Sky, Bajaj Allianz, Dr Reddy's,
  Publicis Sapient, etc.). [Source 1, 2]
- **AI-driven smart wearables** (GOQii Smart Vital / Vital Lite) using AI algorithms
  for continuous heart-rate and SpO2 monitoring, synced to the GOQii app. [Source 3]
- **1-on-1 human health coaching** via certified coaches and doctors inside the app.
  [Source 4]
- **India Fit Report:** a real annual health-data study drawn from millions of GOQii
  app users via Health Risk Assessments (HRA). [Source 5]
- A stated business thesis that preventive corporate wellness delivers measurable ROI
  through reduced absenteeism and improved productivity. [Source 6]

**The fictional project below** imagines GOQii's enterprise delivery team deploying
this platform to one new enterprise client. The project, client, names, and numbers
are invented; the *product, business model, and industry context* are real.

## 2. Business Case

### 2.1 The Problem / Opportunity
"Meridian Logistics Pvt Ltd" (fictional client) is a 5,000-employee Indian logistics
firm facing rising group-health-insurance premiums, growing sedentary-lifestyle health
claims, and falling employee engagement scores after a shift to hybrid work. Leadership
wants a data-driven preventive-wellness program rather than a one-off perk.

### 2.2 The Proposed Solution
GOQii Technologies Pvt Ltd will deploy its AI-powered corporate wellness platform
across all 5,000 Meridian employees over a ~6-month engagement:
- Distribute GOQii AI-enabled smart bands to opted-in employees.
- Onboard employees onto the GOQii app (HRA, activity/sleep/vitals tracking).
- Run the GOQii Corporate Challenge to drive initial and sustained engagement.
- Activate AI + human health-coaching nudges ("Dynamic Motivation" style).
- Deliver an HR analytics dashboard tracking engagement, health-risk distribution,
  and absenteeism trends for Meridian's HR leadership.

### 2.3 Value Proposition & Benefits
- **Quantifiable:** target ≥60% active enrollment, ≥40% sustained 90-day engagement,
  measurable absenteeism reduction, and a downstream case for lower insurance loading.
- **Qualitative:** stronger team morale, employer-brand differentiation, HR data to
  inform future benefits design.

### 2.4 ROI Rationale (Illustrative)
Preventive wellness ROI is framed via avoided costs: reduced absenteeism days, lower
lifestyle-disease claim frequency, and improved productivity — the same logic GOQii
publicly argues for preventive corporate health. [Source 6]
*(All figures in the capstone artifacts are illustrative and self-generated for practice.)*

### 2.5 Stacey Analysis (per Module 1 requirement)
- **Requirements:** moderately agreed (client knows the goal, details emerge) → mid axis.
- **Technology/approach:** largely known (proven GOQii platform) but with SSO and
  integration unknowns specific to client infrastructure.
- **Verdict:** **Complicated, leaning Complex** → justifies a **hybrid** approach:
  *predictive* planning for hardware/logistics/contracts, *adaptive (Scrum)* for the
  app-configuration and engagement-feature workstream. This cleanly satisfies the
  capstone's requirement to produce **both** predictive *and* adaptive artifacts.

## 3. Project Scenario Narrative (Reusable Across All 7 Labs)

**Project name:** VitalEdge — GOQii Corporate Wellness Platform Deployment
for Meridian Logistics Pvt Ltd

**Implementing organization:** GOQii Technologies Pvt Ltd (enterprise delivery team)

**Client organization:** Meridian Logistics Pvt Ltd (fictional)

**Engagement window:** 1 Aug 2026 – 31 Jan 2027 (~6 months) — *fictional dates.*

**High-level phases (predictive backbone):**
1. **Initiation** — Charter, stakeholder register, kickoff.
2. **Planning** — WBS, network diagram, budget, quality/comms/risk plans.
3. **Procurement & Logistics** — GOQii Smart Band sourcing, distribution to 5 sites.
4. **Platform Configuration** — GOQii app setup, SSO, HRA flows, dashboard
   *(run as Scrum sprints)*.
5. **Onboarding & Launch** — employee enrollment, training, Corporate Challenge kickoff.
6. **Operate & Monitor** — engagement tracking, status reports, change requests.
7. **Close-Out** — final report, lessons learned, handover to client HR.
